What DTNT helps you check

The common network questions before the next move.

The value is simple: get oriented faster, make cleaner decisions, and keep useful evidence when the job needs a handoff.

What Wi-Fi is around me?

Survey nearby radios, compare signal posture, and look at channel pressure without opening a separate analyzer.

What devices are on this range?

Scan the active subnet, keep remembered devices visible, and open likely web targets when a local login page exists.

Is the service port open?

Check common TCP ports on a selected host, plus one custom port, when the web path is not obvious.

What is the speed right now?

Run speed checks and keep saved in-app results for comparison, support notes, or proof after changes.

What did this PC remember?

Review saved Wi-Fi profiles and use Pro when you need reveal, copy, and export-oriented workflows.
