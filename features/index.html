What the app covers

Use one shell instead of stitching together separate tools.

These are the product surfaces DTNT is built around today. The goal is not more tabs for the sake of more tabs. It is tighter workflows for Wi-Fi inspection, local-device discovery, selected-host checks, and repeatable proof when you need to show what happened on a network.

Wi-Fi analyzer

Review nearby radios, see signal posture, group the strongest access points by band, and keep the live survey surface readable enough for real field use.

Channel analysis

Focus on one band at a time and compare occupied lanes without the usual clutter of overlapping charts that hide the real decision.

IP scanner

Discover local devices, split infrastructure from endpoints, surface browser-access targets, and probe common service ports on the selected host.

Speed test

Track latency, download, upload, and saved history so you can compare runs, keep proof, and export what matters later.

Profiles

Review Windows saved Wi-Fi profiles, compare live security posture, and keep activation and release details close to the rest of the app.

Operator polish

DTNT is tuned to feel like practical desktop software, with cleaner light and dark themes, tighter states, and calmer detail rails.
