May 2026

Theme, install, and support polish

Light mode is the default first-run experience, with Dark mode still available from the app theme toggle. DTNT also keeps install and support paths focused around Microsoft Store, dt-nt.com, and support@dt-nt.com.

May 2026

Cleaner IP Scanner workflow

IP Scanner improvements make field checks easier to read: remembered devices, online and offline state, custom labels, filtering, web indicators, Copy IP, selected-device port checks, and better active-network detection.

May 2026

Pro workflow improvements

Pro remains focused on the pressure points that matter in the field: best-channel guidance, richer channel detail, selected-host follow-up, port checks, Copy Name and MAC, exports, saved-key actions, continuous ping, and trace route.

May 2026

Device labels and remembered hosts

DTNT keeps remembered hosts more useful between scans and helps labels stay attached to the right device when the network changes.

April 2026

Wi-Fi and channel analysis polish

Wi-Fi and channel views were refined to make nearby radios, channel pressure, signal posture, and best-channel decisions easier to read during real field work.

April 2026

Speed test and profile improvements

Speed test history, saved-profile review, and app wording were tightened so DTNT feels clearer when checking a network, comparing results, or preparing a handoff.

April 2026

Customer help pages

Added clearer customer guidance for features, install, pricing, activation, FAQ, privacy, terms, refunds, and release notes.

April 2026

Desktop app foundation

The Windows app experience was shaped around practical field workflows: dashboard context, Wi-Fi scanning, channel analysis, IP discovery, speed checks, saved profiles, and diagnostics.

Release direction

DTNT stays focused on practical Windows field work: see the Wi-Fi environment, scan the local network, check selected hosts, save useful results, and unlock deeper Pro actions when needed.
